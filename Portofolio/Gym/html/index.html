What is Rennes Fitness

Rennes Fitness is an exchange group for fitness enthusiasts. It doesn't matter whether you are a novice or a veteran, you can find friends here. Train together, play together, waiting for you!

  • Beginner

    For beginners who know little about fitness.You will learn the the basic knowledge here,refer to nutrition,gym equipment use,recovery etc.

  • Intermediate

    Who already have some training experience want to find a met or club, don't hesitate ,just come here!

  • Advanced

    For advanced trainers, we hope you can become a mentor for newcomers and work together to maintain our community harmony!

  • Specialist

    You are specialist of powerling/dancing/Yoga/..., we need you to be our tutor,and you will get a chance to be a coach here!
